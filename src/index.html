What‚Äôs an NFT?

An NFT is a blockchain-based token that proves ownership of a digital item such as images, video files, and even physical assets. Owning an NFT simply means instead of owning an item physically, you own it virtually, on the blockchain.

Lions of Africa is a collection of 1000 unique Illustrated NFTs that will live on the Ethereum blockchain.

What is Minting?

Minting an NFT refers to converting digital files into crypto collections or digital assets stored on the blockchain.

The digital items or files will be stored in a decentralized database or distributed ledger and cannot be edited, modified, or deleted.

How to buy ETH?

Choose a Cryptocurrency Exchange. You can't purchase cryptocurrency through a bank or an online brokerage like Fidelity or Vanguard, so you'll have to use a cryptocurrency trading platform like binance or coin base.

How to connect my Wallet?

Go to OpenSea.io, click on the icon in the top right, and select "My Profile" you'll be prompted (and given instructions) to connect your wallet. When done, Search for the Lions of Africa nft.

How to buy Lions of Africa NFT?

  1. Visit the website https://lionsofafricanft.com.
  2. Click on connect wallet.
  3. Select your desired wallet for mint ( Be sure to have enough Ethereum to cover for gas fees ).
  4. It will take you to your wallet to sign in, then choose connect.
  5. Go back to the website, then select how many NFTS you want to mint.
  6. Click on mint.
  7. Pay the fees.

Congratulations, you successfully minted a one of a kind Lions of Africa NFT! You can view your nft on OpenSea or Etherscan.

How to Sell?

  1. Go to opensea and connect your wallet, then go to your profile, above the upper right hand corner of the page.
  2. Click on SELL.
  3. Put your desired amount on the price of your nft and duration then click on "complete listing", thereby successfully listing your Lions of Africa NFT for sale.
